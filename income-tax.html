Income Tax

Enter income in ₹ (e.g., 1000000 for 10 Lakh). For Old Regime, include deductions (e.g., 80C up to ₹1,50,000, 80D up to ₹25,000, standard ₹50,000). Tax prorated for FY 2025-26 (April 1–March 31).

Tax Regimes (FY 2025-26)

New Regime: Simplified with lower tax rates, only ₹75,000 standard deduction. Ideal for minimal investments.

  • ₹0 – ₹3,00,000: 0%
  • ₹3,00,001 – ₹7,00,000: 5%
  • ₹7,00,001 – ₹10,00,000: 10%
  • ₹10,00,001 – ₹12,00,000: 15%
  • ₹12,00,001 – ₹15,00,000: 20%
  • ₹15,00,001+: 30%

Old Regime: Higher rates but allows deductions (e.g., ₹1,50,000 under 80C, ₹25,000 under 80D, ₹50,000 standard). Suits those with investments or loans.

  • ₹0 – ₹2,50,000: 0%
  • ₹2,50,001 – ₹5,00,000: 5%
  • ₹5,00,001 – ₹10,00,000: 20%
  • ₹10,00,001+: 30%

Notes: Both regimes include 4% cess. New regime is default with minimal deductions. Old regime reduces taxable income via deductions (max ₹2,25,000 here). Surcharges excluded for simplicity.
