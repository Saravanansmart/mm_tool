Frequently Asked Questions

What is an NPS Calculator?

An NPS Calculator is an online tool that estimates the retirement corpus and monthly pension under the National Pension System (NPS) in India, based on monthly contributions, age, expected returns, and annuity purchase percentage.

How is the NPS corpus calculated?

The NPS corpus is calculated using the compound interest formula for monthly contributions over the investment period. At retirement, a minimum of 40% of the corpus is used to purchase an annuity, and the rest is paid as a lump sum.

Who can invest in NPS in India?

Indian citizens aged 18 to 70 years, including salaried and self-employed individuals, can invest in NPS. It is designed to build a retirement corpus through market-linked investments.

Are NPS returns guaranteed for FY 2025-26?

No, NPS returns are market-linked and not guaranteed. The calculator uses an assumed return rate (e.g., 10%) for estimation. Actual returns depend on market performance.
