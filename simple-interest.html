Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Simple Interest Calculator?

A Simple Interest Calculator is an online tool that estimates the interest earned or paid on a principal amount, based on the annual interest rate and duration, using the formula I = P * R * T / 100.

How is simple interest calculated?

Simple interest is calculated using the formula I = P * R * T / 100, where I is the interest, P is the principal amount, R is the annual interest rate, and T is the time in years.

What is simple interest used for in FY 2025-26?

In FY 2025-26, simple interest is used for short-term fixed deposits (e.g., <6 months), certain savings accounts, and some loans where interest is not compounded.
