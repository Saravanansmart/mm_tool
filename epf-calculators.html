Frequently Asked Questions

What is an EPF Calculator?

An EPF Calculator is an online tool that calculates the Employee Provident Fund maturity amount based on monthly salary, contributions, interest rate, and tenure.

How is EPF interest calculated?

EPF interest is calculated monthly at 8.25% per annum (for FY 2025-26) and credited annually, using the formula: Interest = (Previous Balance + Monthly Contribution) * (Interest Rate / 12).

What are the tax benefits of EPF in FY 2025-26?

Employee contributions to EPF are eligible for tax deductions up to ₹1.5 lakh under Section 80C. Interest earned and maturity amounts are tax-exempt if withdrawn after 5 years of service.

What is the EPF interest rate for 2025?

The EPF interest rate for FY 2025-26 is 8.25% per annum, as per EPFO guidelines.

Who is eligible for EPF in India in 2025?

Employees in organizations with 20 or more employees, earning a basic salary up to ₹15,000 per month, are mandatorily covered under EPF. Others can opt in voluntarily.

Can I withdraw my EPF balance in 2025?

Yes, EPF withdrawals are allowed for specific reasons like retirement, unemployment, or home purchase, subject to EPFO rules.

What is the maximum EPF contribution in 2025?

The employee and employer each contribute 12% of the basic salary. For salaries above ₹15,000, the employer’s EPS contribution is capped at ₹1,250/month.

How does EPF help in retirement planning?

EPF provides a secure, long-term savings option with guaranteed interest, ideal for retirement. Use our Retirement Planning Calculator for comprehensive planning.
