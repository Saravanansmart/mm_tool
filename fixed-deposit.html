Frequently Asked Questions

What is an FD Calculator?

An FD Calculator is an online tool that estimates the maturity amount and interest earned for a Fixed Deposit (FD) based on the principal amount, annual interest rate, and tenure.

How is FD maturity calculated?

FD maturity is typically calculated using the compound interest formula with quarterly compounding. For tenures less than 6 months, simple interest may be used, depending on the bank’s policy.

What is the minimum FD amount in India for FY 2025-26?

The minimum FD amount varies by bank, typically starting at ₹1,000 for most banks in FY 2025-26. Post office FDs may start at ₹100. Check specific bank or post office terms before investing.

Is FD interest taxable in FY 2025-26?

Yes, FD interest is taxable under the investor’s income tax slab for FY 2025-26. TDS may apply if interest exceeds ₹40,000 (₹50,000 for senior citizens) annually, as per Income Tax rules.
