What is an FD Calculator?

An FD Calculator is an online tool that estimates the maturity amount and interest earned for a Fixed Deposit (FD) based on the principal amount, annual interest rate, and tenure. It uses standard compound interest formulas to project your final returns.

How is FD maturity calculated?

FD maturity is typically calculated using the compound interest formula with quarterly compounding. This means that every three months, the earned interest is added back to the principal, and the next interest cycle calculates interest on the new, larger balance.
