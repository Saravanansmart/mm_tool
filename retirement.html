FAQs

What is a retirement corpus?

A retirement corpus is the total savings accumulated by retirement to fund post-retirement expenses, accounting for inflation and returns.

How does inflation affect retirement planning?

Inflation increases future expenses, requiring a larger corpus to maintain your lifestyle. A 6% inflation rate doubles costs in about 12 years.

What is NPS in retirement planning?

The National Pension System (NPS) is a government-backed scheme offering tax benefits under Section 80C and 80CCD, with equity and debt investment options.

How much should I save monthly for retirement?

It depends on your current age, retirement age, expected expenses, and returns. Use this calculator to estimate based on your inputs.

When is the ITR filing deadline for FY 2025-26?

July 31, 2025, for non-audit cases. Late filing incurs a ₹5,000 penalty under Section 234F.
