Loan Amortization & Tax Benefit Formulas

Education Loan EMIs are calculated using the standard reducing balance method. The formulas below detail the EMI, the capitalization of moratorium interest (if applicable), and the tax savings under Section 80E.

1. Monthly EMI Calculation

EMI = P_{final} x r x ((1+r)^n}/{(1+r)^n - 1}

2. Moratorium Interest Addition (Capitalization)

Interest accrued during the moratorium period is often added back to the principal ($P$), increasing the loan amount used for EMI calculation ($P_{\text{final}}$).

P_{final}} = P x (1 + r_{ann})^{Moratorium Years}

3. Section 80E Tax Savings

The total interest paid is fully deductible for up to 8 years, yielding tax savings equivalent to your marginal tax slab rate on the eligible interest amount.

Tax Savings = {Total Interest Paid}_{\leq 8 \text{ Years}}) \times \text{Tax Slab Rate}

Key to Variables:

  • $P$: Initial Loan Amount (₹)
  • $P_{\text{final}}$: Principal after Moratorium Interest is Capitalized.
  • $r$: Monthly Interest Rate ($\frac{\text{Annual Rate}}{12 \times 100}$)
  • $n$: Repayment Tenure in Months
  • $r_{\text{ann}}$: Annual Interest Rate (used for Moratorium Compounding)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Education Loan EMI Calculator?

An Education Loan EMI Calculator estimates monthly EMI, total interest, and tax benefits under Section 80E for loans from ₹50,000 to ₹1.5 crore, supporting PM-Vidyalaxmi and CSIS subsidies.

How is Education Loan EMI calculated in 2025?

EMI = [P × r × (1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n - 1], where P is loan amount, r is monthly interest rate, and n is repayment months.

What are Section 80E tax benefits for FY 2025-26?

Interest paid on education loans is fully deductible under Section 80E (old regime) for up to 8 years, with no upper limit.

What is PM-Vidyalaxmi scheme in 2025?

PM-Vidyalaxmi offers collateral-free loans for top 860 institutions with 3% interest subvention for incomes up to ₹8 lakh. Apply by August 31, 2025.

What is CSIS subsidy for education loans in 2025?

CSIS provides full interest subsidy during the moratorium for students with family income up to ₹4.5 lakh. Apply by March 31, 2025.
