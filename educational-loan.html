FAQs

How is education loan EMI calculated?

EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1), where P is loan amount, r is monthly interest rate, and n is repayment months.

What are Section 80E tax benefits?

Interest paid on education loans is fully deductible under Section 80E (old regime) for up to 8 years. No limit on deduction amount.

What is PM-Vidyalaxmi?

A scheme offering collateral-free loans for top 860 institutions with 3% interest subvention for incomes up to ₹8 lakh. Apply by August 31, 2025.
