Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Home Loan EMI Calculator?

A Home Loan EMI Calculator is an online tool that calculates the Equated Monthly Installment (EMI), total interest, and total payment for a home loan based on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

How is Home Loan EMI calculated?

EMI is calculated using the formula: EMI = [P * r * (1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n - 1], where P is the loan amount, r is the monthly interest rate, and n is the tenure in months.

What are the tax benefits on home loans in FY 2025-26?

Home loan borrowers can claim deductions up to ₹2 lakh on interest under Section 24(b), ₹1.5 lakh on principal under Section 80C, and additional benefits under Section 80EEA for first-time buyers.

What is the typical home loan interest rate in India for 2025?

Home loan interest rates in 2025 typically range from 8.5% to 9.5% per annum, depending on the lender and RBI policies.

Who is eligible for a home loan in India in 2025?

Resident individuals, salaried or self-employed, aged 21–65, with a stable income and credit score above 700, are eligible for home loans.

Can I prepay my home loan in 2025?

Yes, prepayments are allowed, but fixed-rate loans may incur penalties up to 2% of the outstanding amount, depending on the lender.

What is the maximum home loan tenure in 2025?

The maximum home loan tenure is typically 30 years, subject to the borrower’s age at loan maturity.

How does home loan EMI affect my budget in 2025?

EMI should ideally not exceed 50% of your monthly income to maintain financial stability. Use this calculator to plan your budget.
