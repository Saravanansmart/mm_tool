FAQs

What is the LTCG tax rate for FY 2025-26?

For equities and mutual funds, LTCG is taxed at 12.5% above ₹1.35 lakh. For property, it’s 20% with indexation or 12.5% without, per Budget 2025.

What is the STCG tax rate for FY 2025-26?

STCG on equities is taxed at 20%. For other assets like property, it’s taxed at your income slab rate.

Can I claim exemptions on capital gains?

Yes, under Section 54 (property reinvestment), 54EC (bonds), or 54F (net proceeds reinvested), subject to conditions.

What is indexation in capital gains?

Indexation adjusts the purchase cost of property for inflation using the Cost Inflation Index (CII), reducing taxable gains. Available for property sold after July 23, 2024.

When is the ITR filing deadline for capital gains?

July 31, 2025, for non-audit cases. Late filing incurs a ₹5,000 penalty under Section 234F.
