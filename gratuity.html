Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Gratuity Calculator?

A Gratuity Calculator is an online tool that estimates the gratuity amount an employee is entitled to in India based on their basic salary, dearness allowance (DA), and years of service. It is applicable for employees with 5 or more years of service.

How is gratuity calculated in India?

Gratuity is calculated using the formula: Gratuity = (15/26) × (Basic Salary + DA) × Years of Service. The years are rounded up, and the tax-exempt amount is capped at ₹20,00,000 for FY 2025-26.

Who is eligible for gratuity in India?

Employees who have completed at least 5 years of continuous service with an employer in India are eligible for gratuity under the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972. This applies to both private and public sector employees.

Is the gratuity amount tax-free for FY 2025-26?

Gratuity is tax-exempt up to ₹20,00,000 or the calculated amount, whichever is lower, for FY 2025-26. Any amount above this limit is taxable. Consult a tax professional for personalized advice.
