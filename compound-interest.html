Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Compound Interest Calculator?

A Compound Interest Calculator is an online tool that estimates the total amount and interest earned on an investment or loan, based on the principal amount, annual interest rate, duration, and compounding frequency.

How is compound interest calculated?

Compound interest is calculated using the formula A = P * (1 + r/(n*100))^(n*t), where A is the total amount, P is the principal, r is the annual interest rate, n is the number of compounding periods per year, and t is the time in years.

What compounding frequencies are available?

The calculator supports monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, and yearly compounding frequencies, allowing you to match the terms of various investments or loans.
